# images/

Real product and lifestyle photography goes here once available. Folders
are pre-created so drops don't require restructuring later:

- `hero/` — large emotional photography for the homepage hero and future
  campaign banners.
- `collections/` — editorial shots for "Colección Aura", "Colección
  Instantes" and any collection added later.
- `packaging/` — unboxing / packaging photography for the "El detalle
  que lo cambia todo" section.
- `products/` — individual product photography for future product pages.

`og-image.jpg` (site root of this folder) is the current Open Graph /
social-share preview image — replace it with a designed asset when one
exists, keeping the 1200×630 size.

Until real photography is shot, the site intentionally uses the brand's
aura-ripple motif and gold line texture instead of stock photos — see
the design note in the main README.
